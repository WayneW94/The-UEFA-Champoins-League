What is the UEFA Champoins League ?

The UEFA Champions League is a European football club competition that involves the champions of every country in Europe. In some European country’s it also includes the top two, three or four clubs based on country rankings, as it stands now Spain, England, Italy and Germany are the four countries in Europe that will included the top 4 teams based on league performance. It is the richest price in European club football as the winner of the competition will be crowd champions Europe.
